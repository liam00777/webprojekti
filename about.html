About StudyNest

StudyNest is a website about a study app idea for students. The app is designed to help students stay organized and motivated.

The idea came from a common problem: students often have many deadlines, assignments and different tools to manage. StudyNest tries to make studying easier by keeping important study tools in one place.

This project website presents the main idea, features and benefits of StudyNest.
