Main Features

StudyNest includes simple tools that can support student life.

AI Study Assistant

Helps students understand topics and gives simple explanations.

Focus Timer

Helps students focus on studying for a set amount of time.

Homework Tracker

Keeps tasks and deadlines in one easy place.

Study Groups

Students can study together and support each other.

Mood Tracker

Helps students notice stress and take care of wellbeing.

Rewards

Students can earn badges and grow a virtual plant or pet.
